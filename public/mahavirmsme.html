MSME BUSINESS: A Clear and Practical Guide

is not just a business manual — it's a powerful source of motivation, clarity, and practical wisdom for every aspiring and existing entrepreneur in India. Whether you're a student with big dreams, a homemaker with creative skills, a rural youth with ambition, or a struggling shopkeeper with a desire to grow — this book lights the path from idea to implementation.

What You’ll Discover in the Book:
  • How to Choose the Right Business Idea that suits your passion, skills, and market demand
  • Step-by-step Planning to turn your idea into a working business model
  • Complete Guide to Government Schemes like PMEGP, MUDRA, and Stand-Up India
  • DPR (Detailed Project Report) creation for funding and loan approvals
  • Legal Compliance Made Simple – registration, GST, licenses, permits
  • System Building for Growth – SOPs, KPIs, HR, inventory, accounting
  • Digital Marketing & Sales on WhatsApp, Facebook, Amazon, IndiaMART
  • Mindset, Motivation, and Leadership strategies to stay focused and resilient

Why This Book is Different:
This is not a theoretical or academic book. It is a hands-on action guide, built from over 25 years of real-world experience in business development, MSME consultancy, and innovation strategy. The author brings powerful insights from working with more than 50 MSMEs, including turning around struggling businesses and designing world-class systems.
More than business techniques, this book gives you the mental strength and inspiration to push past fear, uncertainty, and hesitation. You'll learn how to think like an entrepreneur, how to bounce back from setbacks, and how to lead your team with purpose and confidence.

Trilok Kumar Jha is a seasoned business strategist and MSME mentor with over 25 years of experience in business, finance, management, and innovation. He brings a powerful blend of corporate excellence and grassroots transformation, having spent 15+ years in the corporate sector, where he led complex business operations, systems implementation, and strategic turnarounds.

Over the years, Trilok has empowered more than 50 MSMEs by helping them access funding, reduce operational costs, and implement efficient business process systems. His interventions have included best-in-class inventory and HR practices, helping small businesses become scalable and sustainable.

One of his landmark contributions includes the design and implementation of a world-class Skill Evaluation System, benchmarked with TÜV NORD standards, for the prestigious Reliance Refinery in Jamnagar — the world’s largest refinery.

His transformation work includes turning a loss-making manpower consultancy into a profit-making venture in just 18 months, by streamlining its operations, revamping its services, and re-aligning its workforce strategy.

Through his book “MSME BUSINESS: A Clear and Practical Guide”, Trilok now brings his decades of insight and field experience to aspiring entrepreneurs, small business owners, and growth-stage MSMEs — empowering them to take bold, structured, and successful steps toward business excellence.
